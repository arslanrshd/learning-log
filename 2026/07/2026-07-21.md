### Forex Market Basics

#### 1. What is Forex?

- Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the largest financial market in the world.
- It has a daily trading volume of over **$7 trillion**.
- It involves exchanging one currency for another.

#### 2. Key Pricing Terms

- **Bid:** The highest price a buyer is willing to pay.
- **Ask:** The lowest price a seller is willing to accept.
- **Spread:** The difference between the bid and ask prices.

#### 3. Trading Units

- **Pip:** The smallest price movement of a currency pair.
- **Lot:** The standardized size of a trade.

#### 4. Market Direction

- **Bullish:** Prices are expected to rise.
- **Bearish:** Prices are expected to fall.

#### 5. Important Concepts

- **Liquidity:** The ease of buying or selling an asset without significantly affecting its price.
- **Leverage:** Borrowing funds from a broker to control a larger trading position.
- **Zero-Sum Game:** One trader's profit equals another trader's loss.

#### 6. Market Participants

- Smart Money
- Central Banks
- Governments
- Liquidity Providers
- Retail Traders
